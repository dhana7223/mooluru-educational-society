About Us

Mooluru Educational Trust is a not-for-profit organization founded in 2025 with the vision of transforming lives through education. We believe that every individual deserves access to quality learning, regardless of their background.

Our Vision

To empower communities by making education inclusive, innovative, and impactful.

Our Mission

  • Promote quality education for all age groups.
  • Provide scholarships to meritorious and underprivileged students.
  • Organize skill development and career guidance programs.
  • Engage in community outreach and lifelong learning initiatives.
