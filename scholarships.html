Scholarships

We offer scholarships to deserving students from economically weaker backgrounds, rewarding academic excellence and supporting those in need.

How to Apply

  1. Download the scholarship application form.
  2. Attach required documents (marksheets, income proof, etc.).
  3. Email or submit the form at our office.

For more details, contact our scholarship coordinator at info@mooluruedutrust.org.
